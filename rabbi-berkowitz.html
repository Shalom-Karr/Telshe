Rabbi Berkowitz

Rabbi Berkowitz serves as a 9th Grade Rebbi in the Mechina. Working alongside Rabbi Schwartz, he focuses heavily on the transitional year, instilling a profound Geshmak in learning and serious character development in his young talmidim.
