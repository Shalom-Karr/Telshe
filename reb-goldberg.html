Rosh Yeshiva

Reb Dovid Goldberg, shlit"a

Reb Dovid Goldberg, shlit"a, serves as the Rosh Yeshiva of the Rabbinical College of Telshe. Continuing the golden chain of the Telzer Derech, Reb Dovid leads the Yeshiva with profound Daas Torah, deep Iyun, and unwavering dedication to his talmidim.

Harbotzas Torah

As Rosh Yeshiva, Reb Dovid delivers the highest-level Shiurim Klaliim, guiding the Bais Medrash and Kollel through the most complex Sugyos in Shas. His Shiurim are renowned for their incredible clarity, brilliant lomdus, and the ability to break down the most difficult Rishonim into digestible, profound foundations.

"The Koach of Telshe has always been the Emes in learning. To search for the absolute truth of the Sugya without compromise."

A Father to Talmidim

Beyond his brilliance in learning, Reb Dovid is known for his immense warm heart and genuine care for every single bochur. His door is always open, offering guidance, eitzos, and Chizuk to anyone who needs it, true to the tradition of European Roshei Yeshiva who viewed their talmidim as their own children.
