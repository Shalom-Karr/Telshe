9th Grade Mechina

Rabbi Schwartz

Rabbi Schwartz serves as a 9th Grade Rebbi in the Mechina (High School) division of Telshe Yeshiva. With years of experience in Chinuch, Rabbi Schwartz specializes in transitioning young bochurim into the rigorous, independent environment of Mechina Gemara study.

Educational Philosophy

Shiur Aleph Mechina is a critical juncture in a young bochur's life. Rabbi Schwartz focuses not just on translating the Gemara, but on teaching the bochurim *how* to think—instilling the analytical framework of the "Telzer Derech." His classroom is known for being dynamic, welcoming questions, and fostering a deep love for Torah study.

"Our goal in the Mechina is to give every student the tools to learn independently, and the Middos to live a life of Kiddush Hashem."

Beyond the Classroom

Rabbi Schwartz is deeply involved in the extra-curricular growth of his students. From organizing Shabbos onegs to mentoring students through the challenges of adolescence, he acts as both an educator and a lifelong guide for his talmidim.
