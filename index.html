Our History

Founded in 1875 in Telšiai, Lithuania, the yeshiva was rebuilt from the ashes of World War II by Rabbis Eliyahu Meir Bloch and Chaim Mordechai Katz. Today, it stands as a cornerstone of Torah education in America.

Academic Programs

We offer comprehensive theological studies through our High School (Mechina), undergraduate and graduate Rabbinical programs, and Teachers Seminary, preparing the next generation of Jewish educators and leaders.
