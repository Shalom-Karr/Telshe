A Legacy Reborn

Founded in 1875 in Telšiai, Lithuania, the yeshiva was rebuilt from the ashes of World War II by Rabbis Eliyahu Meir Bloch and Chaim Mordechai Katz. Escaping the destruction of Europe, they replanted the seeds of Torah in Cleveland, Ohio.

Today, standing on a beautiful 57-acre campus in Wickliffe, the Rabbinical College of Telshe serves as a living testament to the eternity of the Jewish spirit and the unbroken chain of transmission.
